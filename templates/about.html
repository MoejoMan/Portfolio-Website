About Me

My name is Joseph Hayes Gonzalez. Computers have always fascinated me, and somewhere along the way that curiosity turned into a love for cybersecurity. I’m on the blue team side of things motsly, figuring out how to keep systems safe, exploring programming, and spreading awareness about safe internet habits to anyone who will listen.

I like getting stuck in with projects. From turning a Raspberry Pi Pico into a Rubber Ducky, to building my Smart CV Analyzer for uni, along with countless other coding projects, to diving into TryHackMe challenges — I learn by doing, experimenting, and sometimes just seeing how far I can push things.

When I’m not fiddling with code or security labs, I’m gaming, thinking about tech, or planning my next project. Down the line, I want to work somewhere I can really make an impact, whether that’s a company, an organization, or for the government, keeping the digital world a little safer along the way.
